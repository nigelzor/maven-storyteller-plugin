storyteller:display-dependency-graph

Full name:

org.highsource.storyteller:maven-storyteller-plugin:0.2-SNAPSHOT:display-dependency-graph

Description:

Display the dependency graph of the current project, or of a specified artifact.

Attributes:

  • Requires dependency resolution of artifacts in scope: test.
  • Binds by default to the lifecycle phase: verify.
